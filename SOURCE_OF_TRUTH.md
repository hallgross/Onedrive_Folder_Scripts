## 💾 Storage — use the external drive, don't run out of space
- **The repo is in the cloud (GitHub).** It is safe no matter what your Mac's disk does — losing local space never loses the work.
- **Keep your LOCAL copy on your external drive `HHDEcoSystem`** (not the Mac's internal disk):
  - Move the `Onedrive_Folder_Scripts` folder onto `HHDEcoSystem`, **or** point OneDrive's sync folder to the external drive (OneDrive Settings → Account → *Choose folder location*), **or** clone fresh onto the drive: `cd /Volumes/HHDEcoSystem && git clone <repo-url>`.
- **If space is tight, Cowork can slim the repo** by removing duplicated media (the videos/soundtrack currently exist in two folders). Ask and it's done — the live site keeps working.
