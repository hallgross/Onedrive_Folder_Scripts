## ✅ Selling Checklist (per ebook)

1. Create the PDF (structure: **scripture → teaching → reflection questions → declaration/prayer**).
2. Design an elegant cover (Canva) — keep the Faith Over Fear brand look consistent.
3. Grab the listing copy from [EDU_Sales_Listings.md](./EDU_Sales_Listings.md).
4. Upload to your store, set price to **$15.99**, attach the PDF.
5. Mark it live and log the URL in your tracking sheet (Content Calendar tab).
