1. **Make the PDF** — open `This_Is_How_You_Walk_on_Water_FULL_BOOK.html` in Chrome → **Print → Save as PDF** (margins Default, Background graphics ON). That's your sellable eBook.
   - *(Optional EPUB later for Kindle/Apple Books.)*
2. **Cover design** — one elegant front cover (Canva), Faith Over Fear brand look: title track, water + gold, "Loretta Hall." Reuse the CH30 / I Rise visual language.
3. **Pick a storefront** (any one that sells digital files): **Payhip, Gumroad, or Etsy** — same platforms the EDU line uses.
4. **Create the listing** — paste from `WOWBOOK_Sales_Listing.md`; set **Digital eBook = $9.99**; upload the PDF; add the cover.
5. **Wire the waitlist → buyers** — the `/wow-book` page already captures "Notify Me." On launch, email that list the buy link.
6. **Go live** — mark it live and log the store URL in the tracker + Command Center.
