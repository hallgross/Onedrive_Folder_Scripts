# WOWBOOK Visual Guardrails — "This Is How You Walk on Water"
*Standing rules for EVERY chapter visual + video frame. Applies to all batches.*
*(This is the memoir/book lane — NOT the WOWB prayer-scroll product lane.)*

## ✅ Every image must be (the 8 review requirements)
1. Feels like real **book/memoir artwork**, not a product image.
2. **Clean title space** at the top.
3. **Consistent style** across the whole series.
4. **Emotional tone matches** the chapter.
5. Woman/girl representation is **respectful, graceful, not overly dramatic**.
6. **No** visible text, mockups, logos, watermarks, distorted hands, strange faces, or confusing symbolism.
7. Palette stays **cream, gold, navy, water-toned**.
8. Works as **both** chapter artwork **and** a video opening frame.

## 🚫 Never (auto-reject / regenerate)
- **Too blurry** — soft atmosphere is fine; muddy/out-of-focus subject is not.
- **Too dark** — sacred light must read; no heavy/oppressive shadow.
- **Too private** — keep testimony dignified, not exposing.
- **Too painful without purpose** — pain only when it carries hope/redemption; no gratuitous suffering.
- **Showing other real people without permission** — use symbolic/anonymous figures, silhouettes, light, or single-subject framing; do not depict identifiable real individuals.
- **Showing addresses, bills, account numbers, medical record numbers, or private documents** — no readable paperwork, charts, numbers, or screens in frame.
