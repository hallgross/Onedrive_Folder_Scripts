## The visual rule (WOWBOOK)
Personal photos are **testimony inserts, not the main artwork for every chapter.**
- **Cinematic artwork = chapter opener** (lives in `06 Visuals for Book/CHxx/`).
- **Personal photos = inside-chapter memory inserts + video overlays** (this folder).
- **Private documents = do NOT use** unless cropped / blurred.
- **No** addresses, account numbers, medical numbers, readable bills, or private family details.
- **No other people's faces** unless Loretta approves.

## Order of use (per chapter)
1. Cinematic artwork opens the chapter.
2. Loretta's approved personal photo(s) appear inside as testimony inserts.
3. Selected photos may also be used as 2–3 sec video overlays.

## 🕊️ Keep these chapters mostly symbolic (sensitive)
CH06, CH07, CH08, CH09, CH10, CH11, CH12, CH13 — use personal photos sparingly and gently; blur/crop anything private.

## How it works
1. Loretta drops photos into the matching `CHxx ...` subfolder.
2. Each photo is tagged (Suggested chapter · What it represents · Use type · Privacy check · Status).
3. Master log: [Real_Testimony_Visuals_Intake.csv](./Real_Testimony_Visuals_Intake.csv).
4. Nothing is placed in the book until **Status = Approved** by Loretta.
