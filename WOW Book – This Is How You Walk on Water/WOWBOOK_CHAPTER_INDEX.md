# 🗂️ WOWBOOK — Chapter Index (Content · Visuals · Videos)
*Everything is matched by chapter number (CHxx). A chapter, its artwork, and its video prompts all share the same CHxx.*

## 📁 How the folders line up (same CHxx everywhere)
```
WOW Book – This Is How You Walk on Water/
├── 01 Manuscript – Chapters/CHxx/   → the written chapter (…_Manuscript_V1_Draft.md)
├── 06 Visuals for Book/CHxx/         → the chapter ARTWORK (…_V1_Draft.png) + its prompt (.md)
├── 10 Marketing – …/CHxx/            → the VIDEO prompts + captions (…_Media_V1_Draft.md)
└── 06 Visuals/08 Real Testimony Visuals – Needs Review/  → your personal photos (e.g. James)
```
