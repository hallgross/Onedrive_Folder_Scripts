YOUR CHAPTER VIDEOS — how to make every chapter play YOUR video
================================================================
The book now plays chapter videos straight from THIS folder. No Higgsfield,
no symbolic clips — just your own approved videos.

TO ADD A CHAPTER VIDEO:
  Put your video in this folder and name it by its chapter number:
      CH01.mp4   CH02.mp4   CH03.mp4  ...  CH30.mp4

That's it. Open the book (with this "11 Videos" folder next to it) and that
chapter's ▶ / "Watch Testimony" plays YOUR video.

- Only add the chapters you have. Missing ones simply won't show a video.
- Already here: CH20.mp4 (your uploaded video) and the I Rise finale.
- Keep the file names EXACTLY as CH##.mp4 (two digits): CH01, CH02 ... CH30.
