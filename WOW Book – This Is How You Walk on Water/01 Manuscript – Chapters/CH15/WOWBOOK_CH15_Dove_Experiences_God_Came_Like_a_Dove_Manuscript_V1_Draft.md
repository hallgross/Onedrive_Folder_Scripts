# CH15 – The Dove Experiences: God Came Like a Dove

**Scripture:** Matthew 3:16 — "...and he saw the Spirit of God descending like a dove, and lighting upon him."
**Theme:** Dove encounters, peace, confirmation, promise, Holy Spirit, God's gentle presence
**Status:** Pending Loretta Approval

---

## Chapter Body

There are some things God sends that you cannot explain away.

You know when it is more than coincidence.

You know when Heaven is speaking.

For me, the dove became one of those signs.

The dove was not just a bird to me. It became a witness. It became a reminder. It became a holy confirmation that God was still with me, still speaking, still covering, still keeping His promise.

Matthew 3:16 says that the Spirit of God descended like a dove.

That scripture helps me understand why the dove felt so sacred. God knows how to come gently. He does not always come with thunder. He does not always come with fire. Sometimes He comes with peace. Sometimes He comes softly enough that only your spirit recognizes it.

And my spirit knew.

When I saw the dove, something in me understood that God had not forgotten me. The dove became a reminder that the promise was still alive. The woman had risen, but the dove was the promise that stayed.

That is what I heard in my spirit:

Divina is the woman that rose.

The dove is the promise that stayed.

And when I think about that, I think about all the seasons where everything around me was changing. People changed. Circumstances changed. My body changed. Money changed. Homes felt uncertain. Relationships shifted. Reports came. Grief came. But the promise stayed.

The dove stayed.

God stayed.

The dove experiences remind me that Heaven can mark your life with signs of comfort. It may not make sense to everybody, but when God sends something to your heart, you know it. You carry it. You remember it. You let it speak when life tries to make you doubt.

The dove was peace.

The dove was presence.

The dove was promise.

And in a life that had known storms, God came like a dove.

## Reader Takeaway
God can send gentle signs of His presence. Not every confirmation is loud; some come softly, like peace landing on the heart.

## Prayer
Holy Spirit, thank You for coming gently. Thank You for every sign of peace, promise, and confirmation You have sent into my life. Help me recognize Your voice when You speak softly. Let me not dismiss the sacred moments that remind me You are near. Let every reader who needs peace receive the comfort of Your presence. In Jesus' name, amen.

## Declaration
God still sends signs of peace.
The promise stayed.
The Holy Spirit is near to me.
I will not dismiss what God used to comfort me.
The dove reminds me that God kept His promise.
