# CH17 – Small Animals in the Kitchen: God Speaks in Strange Places

**Scripture:** Job 12:7 — "But ask now the beasts, and they shall teach thee; and the fowls of the air, and they shall tell thee."
**Theme:** Unusual signs, God speaking through creation, everyday places, spiritual awareness, discernment
**Status:** Pending Loretta Approval

---

## Chapter Body

God does not always speak in places we expect.

Sometimes He speaks in the sanctuary.

Sometimes He speaks through Scripture.

Sometimes He speaks in dreams, visions, prayer, and worship.

And sometimes He will get your attention in a kitchen.

I remember the small animals in the kitchen. It was one of those moments that made me pause because it did not feel ordinary to me. It felt like God was showing me something, using creation to make me pay attention.

Some people might have dismissed it.

Some people might have said, "That is nothing."

But when you walk with God through pain, sickness, loss, and deep waters, you begin to recognize when something is speaking louder than what it looks like on the surface.

Job 12:7 says, "But ask now the beasts, and they shall teach thee; and the fowls of the air, and they shall tell thee."

That scripture reminds me that creation can carry a message. God made everything, and He knows how to use even the smallest thing to awaken discernment.

The animals were not the focus.

God was.

The kitchen was not the focus.

God was.

The unusual moment became a place of spiritual attention. It reminded me that Heaven can interrupt ordinary spaces. God can speak while you are standing in your home, doing regular things, trying to survive another day, and suddenly something catches your spirit.

I have learned not to dismiss the small things.

Sometimes God uses a dove.

Sometimes He uses a giraffe.

Sometimes He uses water.

Sometimes He uses a kitchen moment that nobody else would understand.

And when He does, I want my spirit to be awake enough to hear Him.

This chapter is about discernment. It is about knowing that God is not limited to church walls, microphones, pulpits, or perfect settings. He can speak in strange places, quiet places, painful places, and everyday places.

Even in the kitchen, God was there.

## Reader Takeaway
Do not dismiss the small or unusual places where God may be getting your attention. Creation can carry a message when your spirit is awake to God.

## Prayer
Lord, open my spiritual ears and eyes. Help me recognize when You are speaking, even through small things and unexpected places. Teach me discernment without fear, wisdom without confusion, and awareness without distraction. Let me never limit Your voice to one place or one way. If You speak in the kitchen, in creation, in silence, or in the ordinary, help me hear You. In Jesus' name, amen.

## Declaration
God can speak anywhere.
My spirit is awake to His voice.
I will not dismiss the small signs of God's presence.
Creation can remind me of His wisdom.
Even in ordinary places, God is there.
