# CH14 – The Water Did Not Drown Me

**Scripture:** Isaiah 43:2 — "When thou passest through the waters, I will be with thee…"
**Theme:** Survival, overwhelm, water, healing, storms, God's presence, not being swallowed
**Status:** Approved (Loretta) — 2026-07-03

---

## Chapter Body

There are seasons that feel like water.

Not gentle water.

Not quiet water.

Deep water.

The kind that rises around you until you wonder if you are going to make it through. The kind that touches your breath, your faith, your mind, your body, your home, your family, your finances, and your peace all at once.

I know what it feels like to be in deep water.

I know what it feels like to have wave after wave come, and before you can recover from one thing, here comes another. Sickness. Grief. Rejection. Betrayal. Loss. Reports. Fear. Pressure. Questions. Pain.

But the water did not drown me.

Isaiah 43:2 says, "When thou passest through the waters, I will be with thee…"

That scripture does not say if you pass through the waters.

It says when.

That means God already knew there would be waters. He knew there would be seasons where the ground under me did not feel steady. He knew there would be moments when I would have to trust Him while everything around me was moving.

But He also said, "I will be with thee."

That is the testimony.

Not that I never went through water.

Not that I never cried.

Not that I never felt the waves.

But that God was with me in the water.

The enemy wanted the water to become my grave. God used it to become my testimony. What tried to swallow me became the place where I learned His nearness. What tried to make me panic became the place where He taught me how to breathe. What tried to make me sink became the place where He taught me how to walk.

I did not always feel strong.

But I was held.

I did not always see the shore.

But I was not alone.

I did not always understand the wave.

But God did not let it take me under.

This is why I can say it now:

The water did not drown me.

It witnessed God keeping me.

## Reader Takeaway
The waters may be real, but they are not greater than the God who promised to be with you in them.

## Prayer
Lord, thank You for being with me in the waters. Thank You for not allowing what overwhelmed me to drown me. Teach me how to trust You when life feels deep, unstable, and uncertain. Help me remember that the wave is not greater than Your Word. Strengthen every reader who feels like they are passing through deep waters. Let them know they are not alone, and they will not be swallowed. In Jesus' name, amen.

## Declaration
The water did not drown me.
God is with me in deep places.
The wave is not greater than the Word.
What tried to swallow me became my testimony.
I will pass through because God is with me.
