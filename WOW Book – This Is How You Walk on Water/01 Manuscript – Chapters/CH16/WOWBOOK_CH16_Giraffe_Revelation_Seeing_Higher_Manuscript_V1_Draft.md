# CH16 – The Giraffe Revelation: Seeing Higher

**Scripture:** Habakkuk 2:2 — "Write the vision, and make it plain upon tables, that he may run that readeth it."
**Theme:** Vision, elevation, perspective, seeing beyond the low place, God teaching you to look higher
**Status:** Approved (Loretta) — 2026-07-03

---

## Chapter Body

Sometimes God will use the simplest thing to speak a deep word.

For me, the giraffe became a revelation.

A giraffe does not see from the ground the way other animals do. It has height. It has reach. It has a different view. It can see beyond what is right in front of it, beyond what is low, beyond what is close, beyond what might be blocking the path beneath it.

And I believe God was speaking to me through that.

He was teaching me to see higher.

There were seasons when life wanted me to look only at what was low — the pain, the pressure, the loss, the report, the rejection, the hardship, the things that were happening around me. And when you are in the middle of those things, it is easy to let your vision drop.

But God kept calling my eyes up.

Habakkuk 2:2 says, "Write the vision, and make it plain upon tables, that he may run that readeth it."

That scripture tells me vision matters. What God shows you matters. What Heaven allows you to see is not random. Sometimes the vision is what keeps you moving when the season is trying to make you stop.

The giraffe reminded me that I was not called to see only from the place of pain.

I was called to see from the place of promise.

I was called to lift my eyes.

I was called to look beyond the moment.

I was called to write what God was showing me, even if my circumstances had not caught up yet.

Seeing higher does not mean pretending the low place is not real. It means refusing to let the low place become your only view. It means asking God, "Show me what You see. Help me see past what is hurting me. Help me see past what is pressing me. Help me see past the season I am standing in."

The giraffe revelation reminded me that God can give you vision in the middle of survival.

He can give you perspective while you are still healing.

He can show you height while you are still walking through the valley.

And sometimes the miracle is not that everything around you changes right away.

Sometimes the miracle is that God changes what you are able to see.

## Reader Takeaway
Do not let a low season become your only view. God can give you higher vision even while you are still healing.

## Prayer
Lord, lift my eyes. Help me see higher than the pain, pressure, and fear around me. Give me vision that comes from You. Teach me to write what You show me, trust what You reveal, and keep moving even when my circumstances have not changed yet. Let every reader who feels stuck in a low place receive fresh vision from Heaven. In Jesus' name, amen.

## Declaration
God is lifting my eyes.
I will not let a low season become my only view.
Heaven is giving me higher vision.
I can see beyond what is trying to block me.
I will write the vision and walk by faith.
