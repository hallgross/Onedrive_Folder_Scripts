# Prophetic Interlude: The Birth of Divina
*An unnumbered interlude — placed after Chapter 11 and before Chapter 12. The approved 30-chapter structure remains intact.*

**Status:** New interlude per Loretta's direction — 2026-07-10 (content drawn from her own prophecy journals; no other chapter altered)

---

Some names are chosen.

Mine was given.

On the morning of **July 13, 2000**, the name came — *Divina*. I did not sit down and invent it. I did not search for something that sounded pretty. It was given to me, from the Father, in the quiet where He speaks. That is where **Divina** first came from.

And then God confirmed it in a way I will never forget.

On the early morning of **August 5, 2000**, a noise woke me up. It was the sound of birds — a flapping over me, close, real. Of course I thought a window had been left open. I got up. It wasn't. So I went back to sleep.

But then there was this warm feeling over me.

And it happened again.

The flapping. The warmth. The nearness.

And I knew — I knew then — it was the **Holy Spirit**, hovering over me, giving me peace. The same Spirit that hovered over the waters in the beginning was hovering over me. Not to frighten me. To settle me. To seal something. To say, *I am here, and I have named you.*

That is the birth of Divina.

Not a brand. Not a business idea. A name breathed by God and confirmed by His own presence hovering over me like the wings of a dove.

So when you see the name **Divina**, know that it did not come from marketing.

It came from the Holy Spirit hovering over a sleeping woman, giving her peace, and letting her know she was His.
