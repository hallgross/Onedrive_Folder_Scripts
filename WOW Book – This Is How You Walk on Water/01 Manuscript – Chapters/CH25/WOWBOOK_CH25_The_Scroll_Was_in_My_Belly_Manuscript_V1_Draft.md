# CH25 – The Scroll Was in My Belly

**Scripture:** Ezekiel 3:1 — "Son of man, eat that thou findest; eat this roll, and go speak unto the house of Israel."
**Theme:** The Word inside, birthing the message, scrolls, calling, writing, testimony becoming assignment
**Status:** Pending Loretta Approval

---

## Chapter Body

Before the scroll was in my hand, it was in my belly.

Before it became artwork, before it became product, before it became a title, before it became something someone else could look at — it was first something God placed inside of me.

The scroll was in my spirit.

It was in my prayers.

It was in my tears.

It was in my testimony.

It was in the things I survived and did not have language for yet.

Ezekiel 3:1 says, "Eat this roll, and go speak…"

That scripture reminds me that some messages have to be eaten before they are spoken. Some words have to become part of you before you can release them. Some assignments are not just written on paper; they are written through your life.

That is what happened to me.

God put a scroll in my belly.

He put prayer in me.

He put declarations in me.

He put testimony in me.

He put words in me that sickness could not silence, grief could not bury, betrayal could not steal, and fear could not cancel.

At first, I did not understand all of it. I only knew something was moving in me. Something was being birthed. Something was coming out of the places where I had cried, prayed, suffered, and believed.

The scrolls were not just pretty words.

They were proof.

Proof that God can take what you lived through and turn it into something that helps somebody else live.

Proof that pain can become prayer.

Proof that survival can become declaration.

Proof that what was hidden in your belly can one day become a message in your hand.

This is why I have to protect the book.

This is why I have to protect the scrolls.

This is why I have to remember that my testimony came before the product.

The scroll was not born in a store.

It was born in surrender.

It was born in the secret place.

It was born in the belly of a woman who kept saying yes to God.

## Reader Takeaway
Some messages are not written first on paper — they are carried in the spirit. What God placed inside you may become healing for someone else.

## Prayer
Lord, thank You for what You placed inside of me. Thank You for the words, prayers, declarations, and testimony that were formed in hidden places. Help me carry the message with purity and release it with obedience. Let nothing steal what You put in my belly. Teach me to protect the assignment and honor the testimony. In Jesus' name, amen.

## Declaration
The scroll was in my belly before it was in my hand.
God placed His Word inside of me.
My testimony is becoming assignment.
Pain is becoming prayer.
What God placed in me will come forth.
