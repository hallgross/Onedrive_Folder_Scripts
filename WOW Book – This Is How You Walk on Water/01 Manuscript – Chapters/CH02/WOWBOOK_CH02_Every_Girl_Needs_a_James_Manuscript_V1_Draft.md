# CH02 – Every Girl Needs a James

**Scripture:** Proverbs 18:24 — "A man that hath friends must shew himself friendly: and there is a friend that sticketh closer than a brother."
**Theme:** Covering, protection, safe love, godly support
**Status:** Approved (Loretta) — 2026-07-03

---

## Chapter Body

Every girl needs a James.

Every girl needs someone safe. Someone steady. Someone whose presence does not make her shrink, fear, or wonder if she matters. Every girl needs someone who can see the child inside of her and say, "You are worth protecting."

For me, James represents covering. Not perfection, not a perfect childhood, and not a life without pain — but covering. A reminder that even when a girl is moved from place to place, even when she does not have the photo albums, the family table, or the easy story, God can still place signs of His care along the way.

Being in foster care all my young life meant I did not carry the kind of childhood memories many people carry. I do not have boxes of pictures to prove every season. I do not have a neat album that explains where I was, who held me, or what I survived. But I do have a testimony.

And part of that testimony is this: God knew I needed covering.

A James is not just a name in this book. A James is a symbol of protection. A symbol of someone standing near enough to say, "You are not invisible." A symbol of godly strength that does not harm, but shelters. A symbol of love that does not take from a child, but guards what God placed inside of her.

There are some wounds only God can fully heal. There are some questions only Heaven can answer. But even in the places where my story was not easy, I can look back and see moments where God gave me signs that I was still seen.

Every girl needs a James.

Every girl needs covering.

And when people cannot cover you the way you need, God Himself becomes the covering that never fails.

## Declaration
