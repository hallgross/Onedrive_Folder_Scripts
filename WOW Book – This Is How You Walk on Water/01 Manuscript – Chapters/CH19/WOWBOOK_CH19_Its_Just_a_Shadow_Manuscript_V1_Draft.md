# CH19 – It's Just a Shadow

**Scripture:** Psalm 91:1 — "He that dwelleth in the secret place of the most High shall abide under the shadow of the Almighty."
**Theme:** Fear, shadows, protection, perspective, secret place, God's covering, spiritual confidence
**Status:** Approved (Loretta) — 2026-07-03

---

## Chapter Body

There are some things that look bigger in the dark.

Fear can do that.

A report can do that.

A loss can do that.

A bill, a silence, a closed door, a pain in your body, a betrayal, a delay — all of it can cast a shadow. And when you are already tired, that shadow can look like it has more power than it really has.

But one day I had to remind myself:

It's just a shadow.

Psalm 91:1 says, "He that dwelleth in the secret place of the most High shall abide under the shadow of the Almighty."

That means there is another shadow.

Not the shadow of fear.

Not the shadow of death.

Not the shadow of lack.

Not the shadow of rejection.

The shadow of the Almighty.

And if I am under His shadow, then every other shadow has to bow.

I am not saying the things I faced were not real. Pain was real. Sickness was real. Loss was real. Betrayal was real. Pressure was real. But a shadow is not the thing itself. A shadow can only appear because there is light somewhere.

That changed how I looked at what tried to scare me.

The enemy wanted me to stare at the shadow.

God wanted me to look at the light.

The enemy wanted me to believe the shadow was bigger than the promise.

God wanted me to remember that His covering was bigger than the threat.

There were seasons when the shadow tried to speak loud. It tried to tell me I would not make it. It tried to tell me I was alone. It tried to tell me the report had the final word. It tried to tell me the loss would define me.

But I had to come back to the secret place.

I had to come back under the shadow of the Almighty.

That is where fear loses its voice.

That is where my spirit remembers who is covering me.

That is where I can say, "It's just a shadow."

Because the shadow did not make me.

The shadow cannot keep me.

The shadow cannot name me.

The shadow cannot cancel the light of God over my life.

I am covered by something greater.

## Reader Takeaway
Do not give every shadow the authority of a sentence. If you are under God's covering, fear does not get the final word.

## Prayer
Lord, bring me back to the secret place. Teach me to abide under Your shadow instead of being ruled by the shadows of fear, sickness, loss, or uncertainty. Help me see the light behind what tried to scare me. Strengthen my faith and remind me that Your covering is greater than every threat. Let every reader stop agreeing with fear and start resting under the shadow of the Almighty. In Jesus' name, amen.

## Declaration
I dwell in the secret place of the Most High.
I abide under the shadow of the Almighty.
Fear does not have the final word.
The shadow is not greater than the light.
I am covered by God.
