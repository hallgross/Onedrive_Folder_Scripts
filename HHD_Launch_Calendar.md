## Surprise drops (between monthly launches)
Small, exciting releases that keep momentum without overwhelming the team:
- A new **WOW Book** chapter
- A new **Divina Wings** design
- A special **figurine**
- A limited scripture tee or prayer scroll

## Rhythm rules
- **One main collection per month** (above), prepped ahead so it's polished on launch day.
- **Surprise drops** fill the gaps — announced, not scheduled, to create delight.
- **Gate everything:** a collection only goes live when its products are **LAUNCH READY** (all 10 checks ✓).
- Tie each launch to its email campaign + social graphics (checks 9 & 10) so marketing is ready the same day.
